1. Present Perfect Tense

- Structure: [Subject + have/has + past participle (V3)]

- When do you use?
+ Life experience: Something you have done in your life (no exact time)
Eg: I have been to Paris.
+ Recent actions: Just happend
Eg: He has just arrived.
+ Result in the present: Past action with a result now
Eg: She has lost her keys (so she can't open the door).
+ Unfinished time: Start in the past, still happening
Eg: We have lived here for 5 years.
- Common words with Present Perfect: just, already, yet(negative/question), never, ever, for + time, since point in time
